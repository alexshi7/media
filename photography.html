Background

From a young age the storytelling ability of photography has captured my interest. Starting from high school, I began my journey as a freelance photographer, specializing in portraiture, candid photos, concert photography, and sports photography. Whatever the occasion, I love being able to use my passion to immortalize memories forever.

Technical Information:

Photo editing software: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom
Camera gear: Nikon Z 6, Nikkor Z 70-200mm f/2.8 VR S lens, Nikkor Z 24-70mm f/4 S lens
